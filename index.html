Introducing
A Computing Society That Embraces the Digital Future

Welcome to the CSSL GenZ Chapter of the University of Jaffna, established in 2021. We are thrilled to present a dynamic and forward-thinking community that is dedicated to embracing the digital future. Under the patronage of Dr. Thabotharan, our esteemed guiding force, the CSSL GenZ Chapter at the University of Jaffna strives to create an environment where students can thrive in the ever-evolving field of computing. With a focus on innovation, collaboration, and professional growth, we aim to equip our members with the skills and knowledge necessary to navigate the digital landscape of tomorrow. As a pioneering student chapter of the prestigious Computer Society of Sri Lanka (CSSL), we are committed to promoting IT professionalism and fostering a strong network within the industry. Through a wide range of activities, events, and initiatives, we offer invaluable opportunities for our members to enhance their technical expertise, expand their horizons, and forge meaningful connections with industry experts and fellow enthusiasts.
